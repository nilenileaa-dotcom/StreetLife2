SERVER RULES

Street Life Roleplay

Official Server Rules

Important Notice

All players are expected to read and understand these rules. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse for violation. Failure to comply may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the server.

General Rules

+
  • Respect all players and staff members at all times WARNING/BAN
  • No racism, sexism, homophobia, or hate speech of any kind PERMANENT BAN
  • English must be used in all public chats for communication purposes
  • No advertising other servers or communities PERMANENT BAN
  • Do not exploit game bugs or glitches - report them to staff instead BAN
  • No cheating, hacking, or use of third-party mods that provide unfair advantage PERMANENT BAN
  • Keep out-of-character (OOC) discussions to appropriate channels
  • Follow all instructions from server staff and administrators

Roleplay Rules

+
  • In Character (IC): You must always stay in character while roleplaying. Never use real-life information (OOC) in the game.
  • OOC Chat (Out of Character): This is communication outside of roleplay - server discussions, bugs, IRL topics. Must be clearly marked as OOC and not used in RP.
  • Powergaming: Don't perform actions that would be impossible in real life. BAN
  • Metagaming: Don't use information from outside the game (Discord, streams, etc.) in your roleplay. BAN
  • Fear RP: When threatened with a weapon, you must react realistically and value your character's life.
  • RDM (Random Deathmatch): No killing players without clear RP justification. BAN
  • VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch): Don't use vehicles as weapons to kill players. BAN
  • New Life Rule (NLR): After death and respawn, forget everything that happened. Don't return to the death location immediately. 30-minute cooldown required.
  • Combat Logging: No leaving the server to avoid police or when about to die/be arrested. BAN
  • Cop Baiting: Don't troll police without valid RP reasons. Respect officers appropriately.
  • Mass RP: Large-scale events (riots, heists, police raids) must be realistic - imagine the city is full of people.
  • Toxic Player: No excessive trolling, insulting players without reason, or OOC insults. BAN
  • No Hacking/Exploits: No using bugs, cheats, or scripts. PERMANENT BAN
  • Excessive Complaining: Don't constantly complain about actions - no crying to police or staff about normal game events.
  • Poor Reason to Kill: No killing players with weak/unrealistic justifications.
  • Lies to Staff: Never lie to administrators. PERMANENT BAN
  • IC to OOC Problems: Don't bring in-game conflicts into out-of-game disputes.
  • Weapon Usage: Know how to properly use your weapons. If inexperienced, don't carry high-level weaponry.
  • CCTV Responsibility: If you commit crimes in monitored areas with cameras, you accept the consequences when police review footage.
  • Speed Running: No rushing character objectives or farming activities to complete them unrealistically fast. RP takes time.
  • Faction Logs & Cooldown: All illegal organizations must maintain Discord servers with announcement and attack logs. Defense logs must be recorded. Minimum 1-hour cooldown between major actions.
  • Robbery Situations: If outnumbered 2v1 or 3v1 with weapons, you cannot shoot them all or escape. You must comply and RP the situation.
  • Police Chase: When pursued by PD and caught, you must comply. Illegal teams can choose to engage, but no admin spamming.
  • PK (Player Kill): If killed by someone, don't return to RP with them unless they initiate.
  • FK (Faction Kill): If killed by a faction/organization, don't return to RP with them.
  • CK (Character Kill): Permanent character death - serious RP consequence.

Faction Rules

+
  • Faction leaders must maintain active rosters and report changes to staff
  • No cross-faction communication without proper in-character reason
  • Follow faction hierarchy and chain of command
  • Faction activities must remain realistic and immersive
  • No faction hopping without proper RP justification and staff approval
  • Official factions must follow their designated roleplay guidelines
  • Criminal factions may not exceed realistic member limits
  • Law enforcement factions must follow proper procedure and escalation

Business & Economy Rules

+
  • Business owners must maintain realistic operations and hours
  • No exploiting business systems for unfair economic advantage BAN
  • Business conflicts must be resolved through roleplay, not OOC disputes
  • Follow all server economy guidelines and pricing standards
  • No money laundering or unrealistic financial practices BAN
  • Business transactions must be properly roleplayed
  • Illegal businesses must operate with realistic risk and consequences

Communication Rules

+
  • Use appropriate voice chat ranges for realistic communication
  • No excessive screaming, mic spam, or disruptive audio MUTE
  • Radio communications should be used realistically by appropriate factions
  • Keep out-of-character (OOC) chat in designated channels only
  • No trolling, harassment, or disruptive behavior in voice or text chat MUTE/BAN
  • Use proper roleplay language - avoid modern slang or memes in character

Vehicle Rules

+
  • Drive realistically - no excessive speeding in populated areas
  • No vehicle exploiting or duplication BAN
  • Park vehicles in appropriate locations, not blocking roads or entrances
  • Use turn signals and follow basic traffic laws in roleplay
  • Vehicle modifications should remain within realistic limits
  • No intentionally destroying vehicles without proper RP reason
  • Stolen vehicles must be used with realistic caution
